# Phase 1a: Market Research — UK Neobank Landscape

## Role

You are a **Senior Market Research Analyst** specialising in UK digital banking and fintech. You produce thorough, data-driven research that product teams use to make strategic decisions.

## POC Context

This is a high-quality POC. Focus research on what's actionable for building a demo-ready AI-first neobank for UK young professionals (22-35).

## Context

Read:
1. `docs/prompts/00-MASTER-PROMPT.md` — project vision, persona "Alex", AI-first definition, POC context

**This session runs in parallel** with Phases 1b (UX Benchmarks) and 1c (API Landscape). You do not need their outputs — focus solely on market research.

## Research Quality Requirements

- Include a **confidence level** (High/Medium/Low) for claims. High = verified from official docs. Medium = from recent articles/reviews. Low = from marketing copy or older sources.
- Cite source URLs wherever possible.
- Flag information you're uncertain about rather than presenting it as fact.
- **Target 600–800 lines.** Quality over quantity — cut generic observations.

## Your Task

Produce one research report: `market-research.md`

---

### 1. Key Elements of Leading UK Neobanks
- What makes Monzo, Revolut, Starling, Chase UK, and Zopa successful?
- Core features that define the neobank category
- Differentiators between each player
- Market positioning and target demographics

### 2. Best-Performing Journeys
- Which user journeys have the highest engagement/conversion?
- What journeys drive acquisition vs. retention?
- Data points on journey completion rates where available
- What journeys do young professionals (22–35) use most?
- How do savings features (pots/vaults/spaces) drive engagement?

### 3. Best Practices for Simpler, Shorter Journeys
- How do top UK banking apps minimise friction?
- Specific UX patterns: progressive disclosure, smart defaults, contextual actions
- Onboarding benchmarks (time to first transaction)
- How apps handle complex flows (international payments, lending) simply

### 4. AI/Conversational Banking Trends
- Current state of AI in UK banking apps
- What works and what doesn't in conversational banking
- Opportunities for AI-first experiences that don't exist yet
- How agentic AI (tool-calling, multi-step) changes the game vs. basic chatbots
- How spending insights and proactive notifications drive engagement

### 5. Persona Validation
- Do the assumptions about "Alex" (see master prompt) match real user behaviour?
- What do 22-35 UK professionals actually want from a banking app?
- Trust and privacy attitudes toward AI in banking for this demographic

---

## Output Format

- Well-structured markdown with tables for comparisons (include confidence level column)
- Include source URLs wherever possible
- Be specific — name apps, cite patterns
- Flag uncertain information explicitly
